## Campaign argument

> Innovation compounds when every experiment leaves behind one of three assets: a reusable capability, reusable evidence, or a fast no.

The campaign uses a role-derived **GenAI Proving Ground** and the operating sequence:

**FRAME → BOUND → BUILD → STRESS → COMPOUND**

The visual experience is a calibration rig rather than a generic dashboard. A proof specimen advances through five gates, the system accumulates state, and the result resolves into an explicit experiment asset. The interaction is reinforced by an outcome console and a five-factor decision lab.

## Visual and motion contracts

- **Signature hero:** GenAI Proving Ground calibration rig.
- **Role-derived motion:** specimen moves through FRAME, BOUND, BUILD, STRESS, and COMPOUND; station and output states accumulate.
- **Meaningful interaction:** proof-cycle control, outcome-state tabs, and decision-lab sliders.
- **Responsive art direction:** horizontal desktop rig becomes a vertical mobile proof path; navigation becomes an explicit menu.
- **Reduced motion:** animated sequences stop and the complete proof state is presented immediately without removing content or controls.
