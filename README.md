## Brand intelligence and provenance

The campaign is employer-aware without presenting itself as an official KPMG publication.

- **Official logo and corporate source:** https://kpmg.com/xx/en.html
- **Official careers source:** https://kpmg.com/xx/en/careers.html
- **Visual cues observed:** saturated blue-led identity, purple/light-blue accents, white and light-neutral surfaces, dark navy text, large sans-serif headlines, modular editorial panels, restrained line/icon geometry, and professional people-at-work imagery.
- **Campaign palette:** original employer-adjacent colors used for this candidate campaign; the CSS values are not represented as official KPMG brand tokens.
- **Logo decision:** the official KPMG logo is not embedded. This avoids implying endorsement or making a public candidate site resemble an official KPMG publication.
- **Photography decision:** no KPMG photography is copied or hotlinked. The proving-ground visual system is original and derived from the work of the role.

See `brand-intelligence.md` for the full record.
